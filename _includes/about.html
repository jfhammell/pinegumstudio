Melissa Hammell's great-grandmother was a medicine woman from Kitigan Zibi First Nation. Melissa has been active in the indigenous community. She was a member in a native women's drum group. With the Wabano Centre for Aboriginal Health, Melissa held various positions from team lead to director over a period of 10 years before starting Pine Gum Studio. She is Vice-President of the Board of Directors for the Tewegan Housing for Aboriginal Youth.
